Esfera Chess – Support

Thank you for playing Esfera Chess by Squz!

How do the pieces move?

Esfera Chess is played on a geodesic sphere with 132 cells instead of a flat 8x8 board. Pieces follow the same basic rules as standard chess (kings, queens, rooks, bishops, knights, pawns) but adapted for the curved surface. The in-app Tutorial walks you through all piece types in 10 interactive steps — select it from the Learn menu.

How does online multiplayer work?

Tap Play Online to enter matchmaking. You'll be paired with another player at a similar rating. Two time controls are available: Blitz (3+2) and Rapid (10+5). Your rating updates after each game.

What is correspondence play?

Correspondence games are asynchronous — you and your opponent take turns over hours or days. Choose a time control (1, 3, or 7 days per move) and you'll be notified via push notification when it's your turn.

How do I recover my account on a new device?

Use Sign in with Apple on your profile screen to link your account. Once linked, you can sign in on any device to restore your rating, game history, and active games.

The app isn't working as expected

Try the following:

  • Close and reopen the app
  • Ensure your iOS version is up to date (iOS 16+ required)
  • Check your internet connection for online features
  • Restart your device
  • Reinstall the app if issues persist

I'm not receiving push notifications

  • Check that notifications are enabled for Esfera Chess in iOS Settings
  • Ensure you have an active correspondence game with a pending opponent move
  • Notifications are only sent when the app is in the background or closed
