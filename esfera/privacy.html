Privacy Policy – Esfera Chess

Effective Date: March 3, 2026

Esfera Chess by Squz is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy describes how we handle information collected through the app.

Information We Collect

Esfera Chess collects only anonymous analytics data. No personal information leaves your device.

  • Anonymous Analytics: We send anonymous usage signals (app launched, game started, game ended) to TelemetryDeck for aggregate analytics. No personal data is included — no device identifiers, names, or email addresses are transmitted. See TelemetryDeck's privacy policy.

Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, physical location, photos, contacts, device identifiers, or any other personally identifiable information. We do not use advertising identifiers or share data with advertisers. No data is stored on any server — all game data stays on your device.

No Accounts or Server Communication

Esfera Chess does not require an account, internet connection, or sign-in of any kind. There is no server registration, no user profiles, and no cloud storage. The app works entirely offline.

Children's Privacy

Esfera Chess does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13. The app is suitable for all ages.

Third-Party Services

  • TelemetryDeck: Anonymous, privacy-first analytics. No personal data is transmitted. Privacy policy.

Future Updates

Online multiplayer features are planned for a future update. When available, this privacy policy will be updated to describe any additional data collection (such as player handles, game data, and push notification tokens). No new data will be collected without updating this policy first.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy, leave a review on the App Store or use the in-app Feedback form (available in a future update).
