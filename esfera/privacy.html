Privacy Policy – Esfera Chess

Effective Date: March 1, 2026

Esfera Chess by Squz is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy describes how we handle information collected through the app.

Information We Collect

  • Device Identifier: A random identifier generated on your device is used for anonymous authentication. It is not linked to your name, email, or Apple ID.
  • Player Handle: A display name you choose, visible to other players in multiplayer games and leaderboards.
  • Country: Auto-detected from your device locale for leaderboard filtering. You can change it at any time.
  • Game Data: Moves, ratings, streaks, and match history are stored on our server to enable online multiplayer and leaderboards.
  • Push Notification Token: If you grant notification permission, your device token is stored to send correspondence game alerts. You can disable notifications at any time in iOS Settings.
  • Anonymous Analytics: We send anonymous usage signals (app launched, game started, game ended) to TelemetryDeck for aggregate analytics. No personal data is included. See TelemetryDeck's privacy policy.

Sign in with Apple (Optional)

You may optionally link your account using Sign in with Apple. We only receive and store the opaque user identifier provided by Apple. We do not receive your name or email address. This is used solely to let you recover your account on a new device.

Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, physical location, photos, contacts, or any other personally identifiable information. We do not use advertising identifiers or share data with advertisers.

Data Storage

Game data is stored on our server hosted on Oracle Cloud Infrastructure in Melbourne, Australia. Data is transmitted over HTTPS/TLS. We retain your data for as long as your account exists. There is no way to link your game data back to your real identity.

Children's Privacy

Esfera Chess does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13. All identifiers are anonymous and device-generated. The app is suitable for all ages.

Third-Party Services

  • TelemetryDeck: Anonymous, privacy-first analytics. No personal data is transmitted. Privacy policy.
  • Apple Push Notification Service: Used to deliver correspondence game alerts. Governed by Apple's privacy policy.
  • Sign in with Apple: Optional account linking. Governed by Apple's privacy policy.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
