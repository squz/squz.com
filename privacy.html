Privacy Policy – Your World

Effective Date: June 29, 2025

Your World by Squz is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy outlines how we handle information collected through the app.

Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect or request any personally identifiable information such as your name, email address, phone number, or physical location.

Information We Do Collect

  • Anonymous Usage Data: We collect non-identifying usage statistics (such as which features are used most) to improve the app.
  • Crash Reports: We collect anonymised crash logs to fix bugs and improve stability.
  • Game Center (Optional): If you choose to use Apple’s Game Center for leaderboards and achievements, Apple’s privacy policy applies to that service. We do not access your Game Center ID or related personal data.

Children's Privacy

Your World is designed to be appropriate for children under 13. We do not collect personal data from children, and all analytics are anonymised and aggregated. The app complies with the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Firebase (by Google): For anonymised crash reports and basic usage analytics. Firebase may store anonymised data on servers outside your country. You can read their privacy policy here: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy
  • Apple Game Center: Used optionally for achievements and leaderboards. Governed by Apple’s privacy policies.

Data Storage and Retention

We retain only anonymised data and do not store any user-specific or device-linked information. Data is stored securely by our analytics providers and used only for app performance improvement.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or your child’s use of the app, please contact us at: hello@squz.com
